About

Strategic Ownership.
Enduring Value.

Eliud Holdings is a privately held parent organization established to acquire, own, and scale businesses across multiple sectors. We operate as a non-operational parent, focusing on governance, capital allocation, and strategic growth.

Our objective is to build a diversified portfolio of profitable, resilient companies that generate sustained value over decades.

"We do not chase trends. We compound value. We operate with discipline, clarity, and restraint."

— The Eliud Standard

Governance

Our Code
of Conduct

How we work, lead, and serve — across every subsidiary and community we touch.

This Code applies to every Director, employee, contractor, and partner who acts on our behalf.

01

Integrity

Honest dealing, no corruption, and clear management of conflicts of interest.

02

People

Equal opportunity, dignity at work, and zero tolerance for forced or child labour.

03

Stewardship

Protect data and company assets; respect Ghana's environmental laws in all we do.

04

Speak Up

Report concerns in good faith without fear — confidentially via our Ethics Hotline.

"We hold ourselves to the highest standard — not because the law demands it, but because the communities we serve, the employees we lead, and the investors who trust us deserve nothing less."
